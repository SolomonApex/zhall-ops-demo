Specialty Cocktail

The Z Hall Signature
Featured cocktail for the Johnson-Mitchell Wedding
  • 1.5 ozElderflower gin
  • 3 ozProsecco (chilled)
  • 3 slicesFresh cucumber (muddled)
  • 0.5 ozSimple syrup
  • GarnishCucumber ribbon + edible flower
Method: Muddle cucumber in shaker. Add gin and simple syrup with ice. Shake briefly. Strain into chilled flute or coupe. Top with chilled prosecco. Do not shake prosecco. Garnish with cucumber ribbon. Serve immediately.
